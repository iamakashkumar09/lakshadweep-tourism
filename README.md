This repository contains the source code for the **Official Lakshadweep Tourism Website**, developed to provide reliable, updated, and accessible information to tourists and citizens.  
The platform aims to promote tourism, enable transparent dissemination of information, and ensure a seamless digital experience for users.

This project follows Government of India standards for accessibility, performance, and security.

---

## 1. Purpose of the Project

- To provide verified information regarding the islands, tourist locations, activities, and guidelines.  
- To support digital governance and enhance service delivery under the Digital India initiative.  
- To maintain an efficient, transparent, and secure tourism communication platform.  
- To enable digital interactions such as enquiries, feedback, and information requests.

---

## 2. Technology Stack

- **Next.js (App Router)**
- **React (JavaScript)**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **Node.js**
- **Backend Route Handlers (Next.js API)**
- Optional:
  - **MongoDB / PostgreSQL** for dynamic data
  - **Cloud storage** for assets
